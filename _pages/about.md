I am an incoming M.Eng. student in **Integrated Circuit Engineering** at the [School of Integrated Circuits, Southeast University](https://ic.seu.edu.cn/), beginning in Fall 2026. I will join the **Energy-Efficient Electronics and Design Automation (E3DA) Lab** under the supervision of Prof. Yarui Peng.

I received my B.Eng. degree in **Integrated Circuit Design and Integrated Systems** from Wuhan University of Technology in 2026. I ranked **1st out of 35 students** in my major, with a GPA of **4.285/5.0**.

My research interests lie at the intersection of **electronic design automation (EDA)**, **design for testability (DFT)**, and **advanced packaging**. I am particularly interested in power delivery and routing optimization for 2.5D/3D chiplet systems, as well as test-access architectures and scheduling methods for heterogeneous integrated systems.

Research Interests
======

- EDA algorithms for 2.5D/3D chiplet and advanced-package design
- Power delivery network, RDL routing, and multi-physics-aware optimization
- DFT, fault simulation, and IEEE 1838-based test access architectures
- Optimization algorithms and reproducible EDA software

Selected Highlights
======

- Ranked **1/35** in Integrated Circuit Design and Integrated Systems at Wuhan University of Technology
- Recipient of the **National Scholarship** and two-time **Outstanding Student** award
- **National Second Prize**, 9th National College Student IC Innovation and Entrepreneurship Competition, 2025
- Engineering experience spanning **RTL/DFT**, **ATE test development**, and **C++ EDA tool development**

Current Focus
======

I am currently strengthening my foundations in C++, optimization, advanced-package physical design, and DFT. My near-term goal is to develop physically grounded and reproducible methods that connect package planning, power integrity, and testability.
